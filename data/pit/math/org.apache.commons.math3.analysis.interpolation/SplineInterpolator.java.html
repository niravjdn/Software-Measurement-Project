/**

29

30

32

33

34

35

36

37

38 
 * </p>
39 
 * <p>
40

41

42

43

44 
 *  "match up" at the knot points, as do their first and second derivatives).</li>
45 
 * </ol>
46 
 * <p>
47

48

49 
 * </p>

 *
